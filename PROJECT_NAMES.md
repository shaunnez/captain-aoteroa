# Proposed Project Names

Alternatives to "Caption Aotearoa":

1. **Reo Live** — "Reo" means language/voice in Te Reo Māori. Clean, bilingual, and immediately implies real-time broadcasting.

2. **Kōrero** — "To speak/talk" in Te Reo Māori. Simple, memorable, and culturally grounded without being clunky.

3. **Whakarongo** — "To listen" in Te Reo Māori. Shifts the framing toward the audience experience — accessibility-first.

4. **KupuStream** — "Kupu" means word in Māori. Combines the linguistic identity with the streaming nature of the app.

5. **OpenReo** — Signals open access (no app install) and multilingual support ("reo" = language). Pairs well with a community-focused product.
